Projects

From repair services to custom industrial grade developments, our multidisciplinary team solves specialized requirements and needs of the industry.

Counting system based on artificial intelligence (I.A.)

Counting system used in one of the most important parcel companies in the world. It was developed to determine the number of packages that are processed daily.

The I.A. used can be adapted to count practically any type of object on production lines.


Body inspection system

Inspection system used in the quality control of the bodies.

The software developed by Terzett Technologix reconstructs the body parts using multiple ultra-high resolution images, thus allowing the inspector to see defects in the paint.

It has the capacity to store up to 6 months of production.

QR encoding, printing and decoding system

Terzett Technologix developed the software and complete KBA printer integration solution to determine whether promotional QR codes on printed toy brand brochures can be read by end users with their smartphones.

Unlike other common systems that reconstruct printed code, Terzett Technologix's software determines whether the code will actually be readable by a mobile device for everyday use.

Ampoule inspection system

Inspection system used in the quality control of glass products.

Following its successful implementation in more than 100 production lines, this system has been enhanced to be a quality assurance tool for the pharmaceutical industry.

It has the ability to configure and adapt to multiple requirements and recipes. Inspect 50 properties in a 360 ° view of the product


Character analysis system

Terzett Technologix created a set of lamps and a specific lighting system for ultra-reflective materials used in the cosmetics industry.

Derived from optomechanical engineering, it was possible to implement a vision system that made it possible to inspect the cosmetic characteristics of the logos.

Terzett Technologix brand lightings have proven more than 26,280 hours of continuous work with the best performance.


On-board monitoring devices and ammunition counting

High precision system based on military regulations for counting shrapnel and rockets on board the aircraft.

Industrial and aeronautical grade devices used allow the display of monitoring information on a DLP.

Currently the devices used, which have specific routines for microcontrollers developed by Terzett Technologix, are being used in new high-end products.

Production tracking system

A production tracking monitoring system was developed. In this case, our software was adapted to determine the actual polishing cycles that the plant operators are performing.

Distributed systems display information locally and also communicate all the data to a data concentrator system that stores reports for monitoring and displays general graphics.

The software contains a first stage of gamification that is focused on improving productivity.

Artificial vision for detection of packaging defects

High resolution system that allows to distinguish deformities in the 40 micron plastic. Additionally, the algorithms used can determine the circularity of each of the pieces.

The developed solution can perform the necessary operations even considering that the bottom and cover of the plastic packaging are made of highly reflective materials.
